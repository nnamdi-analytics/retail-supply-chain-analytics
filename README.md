# Retail Supply Chain & Inventory Performance Analysis

Retail sales and inventory analytics project using SQL, BigQuery, and Looker Studio.

## Project Overview
This project analyzes retail sales and inventory data to identify revenue-driving products, demand trends, and potential stockout risks.

The analysis was performed using SQL in Google BigQuery and visualized in Looker Studio.

The goal of this project is to simulate real-world retail analytics similar to what operations and supply chain teams perform in large retail organizations.

---

## Business Problem
Retail managers need visibility into product demand, revenue performance, and inventory coverage to ensure product availability and maximize sales.

Without proper analysis, companies risk:
- stock shortages
- excess inventory
- missed revenue opportunities

This project analyzes sales and inventory data to help management make better operational decisions.

---

## Key Business Questions
1. Which products generate the most revenue?
2. Which product categories drive the highest sales?
3. Which regions or fulfillment centers process the most orders?
4. Which products show high demand but low inventory levels?
5. Which products should management prioritize for replenishment?
